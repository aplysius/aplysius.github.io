How small can life get?

Bacteria can be really small, but there seems to be a lower limit. The reasons for this could help understanding what life actually is.

Introduction to what a "living system" is and why this is interesting. What is live? What is a system? What is a living system? Living things are highly complex systems that show some properties, such as reproduction, energy dissipation and stability in space and time. These objects are highly complex, as they are constructed of many many molecules. Thus, I refer to them as Systems.
